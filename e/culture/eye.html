The Eye Of Greater Venith

The Venithian eye is the official logo of Greater Venith. It was made on complete accident while trying to design a new flag for the at-the-time oligarchy. Due to Digital Cheese's enjoyment of the design, alongside the support of various members of the COUNCIL and other administrator positions, it ended up becoming a permanent part of Venithian culture.

Eye Of The People



Created on April 26th 2025 A.D., this eye was admittedly a bit "pixelated" or "blurry" per se, because it was a very small part of a flag and, for all original intents and purposes, was not even meant to be created in the slightest. Above is a transparent version of the logo, however, those wanting to find the *original* intended look can visit this image, which is 3072x2048. As of June 27th, 2026 A.D., this green and purple eye is now called "The Eye Of The People", since it is the primary eye held by us all. Also, there is a higher quality eye in the works, which will make the eye far more impressive on larger images while also fixing up the imperfections, imperfections of which that do NOT add to the soul of V.

Edits Of The Original

On Feburary 22nd 2026 AD, the first edits of the original were made.

Red & Blue Eye



The eye of war was the first of three edits created on Feburary 22nd, 2026 A.D., and it was this eye which kicked off the trend of editing eyes depending on the movement, a practice now ingrained in Venithia herself.

Blue & Yellow Eye



Made on the same day as the eye of war, it is currently unused. However, Digital Cheese the EMPEROR has begun to consider making it the eye of assistance. Until further notice, this eye is still unused as previously mentioned, though.

Animated Eye



This is an animated form containing the original, red+blue, and blue+yellow eyes.

Helena's Eye



This is the Venithian eye for a character of DraconidJoashu's. The eye itself was produced by Digital Cheese.

Inverted Eye
