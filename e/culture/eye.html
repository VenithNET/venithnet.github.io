The Eye Of Greater Venith

The Venithian eye is the official logo of Greater Venith. It was made on complete accident while trying to design a new flag for the at-the-time oligarchy. Due to Digital Cheese's enjoyment of the design, alongside the support of various members of the COUNCIL and other administrator positions, it ended up becoming a permanent part of Venithian culture.

The Original Eye

Created on April 26th 2025 AD, this eye was admittedly a bit "pixelated" or "blurry" per se, because it was a very small part of a flag and, for all original intents and purposes, was not even meant to be created in the slightest.

Edits Of The Original

On Feburary 22nd 2026 AD, the first edits of the original were made.

Red & Blue Eye



The red and blue Venithian eye was the first of these edits, but it would not be the only one of that day. It is currently adopted as the eye of war.

Blue & Yellow Eye



This eye was blue and yellow, it is mostly unused although it was made on the same day as the red and blue versions of the Venithian eye.

Animated Eye



This is an animated form containing the original, red+blue, and blue+yellow eyes.

Helena's Eye



This is the Venithian eye for a character made by DraconidJoashu, however, the eye itself was produced by Digital Cheese.

Inverted Eye Of Venith



This is just the standard eye of Venith, but inverted. Nothing major.
