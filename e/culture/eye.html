The Eye Of Greater Venith

The Venithian eye is the official logo of Greater Venith. It was made on complete accident while trying to design a new flag for the at-the-time oligarchy. Due to Digital Cheese's enjoyment of the design, alongside the support of various members of the COUNCIL and other administrator positions, it ended up becoming a permanent part of Venithian culture.
