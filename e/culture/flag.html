The Flag Of Greater Venith

The Flag of Greater Venith has arguably been in development for years, but the first technical "flag" of V per se was on April 26th, 2025 A.D., and is actually the reason that the eye of Venith exists. The current flag was made on December 12th, 2025 A.D.

The Current Flag



As of June 24th, 2026 A.D., this is our current flag. It is purple and green with a black spider in the middle, its eye is the official eye of our glorious V.
